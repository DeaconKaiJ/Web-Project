League of Legends - Need to know

The information within this page should be used by players who are not total beginners, we recommend that if you are a total beginner then please visit our guides page. The meta is what is working really well right now in League of Legends, some techniques however can be challenging and require some skill so that is why we dont recommend it for completely new players.


Top Lane - Meta

For the top lane, right now in games lane bullies and split pushers are working really well. The early game can create such as snow ball effect on the game that if you can bully your opponenet out of lane and gain a considerable lead, you can often win the game rather quickly. In contrast, games that last a long time can be dominated by pressure on side lanes from great split pushers. Your choice on whether to pick a lane bully or split pusher should rely on your enjoyability of that pick as well as what the enemy picks. For this top lane we recommend, Sion and Yorick for split pushing. For lane bully, we recommend Quinn and Fiora.



Jungle - Meta

With this meta being so heavily influenced by early game pressure, we recommened you play a jungler that excels early game. Your role will be to pressure the lanes as much as possible in contrast to the old meta of farming. Assassins are doing really well as well as some high mobility tank picks. For the Assassin we recommend Wukong and Diana. For the tank picks we recommend, Volibear or Rammus.



Mid Lane - Meta

The mid lane is the one role with never a clear defined meta, with both adc and top lane champs being included in the mid lane, it is a mess of a lane for meta. Typically for the mid lane, it is recommened you play what you can however few champs are doing really well. We recommened Annie, Veigar, Yasuo and Tristana.



ADC - Meta

The ADC meta is a complete mess, there are less attack damage based champions doing well right now than their attack damage counter part. There are still a few hypercarry adcs to pick which can do amazing in the game however they are difficult champions. The safer picks are the non attack damage champs. For this role we recommend Twitch, Vayne, Swain and Veigar.



Support - Meta

Heals, heals, heals. That is support right now in this meta. Tanking or grab picks still have their place if you can play it correctly but healing champions are big right now. Time in lane is really important and a bad trade can be completely turned around with a healing support. For this support role we recommending Soraka, Nami, Janna or Renata Glasc.





Key Terms


Lane Bully- A champion that excels in the early game and trading with their opponenet


Split Pusher- A champion that prefers to push side lanes in the match instead of fighting with the team


Hypercarry- A champion that will deal exceptional damage given the opportunity, enough to solo carry a team


Grab picks- A champion with a grab ability such as Thresh or Blitzcrank.
