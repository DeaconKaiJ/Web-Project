League of Legends - A guide of the basics

So you have decided to play League of Legends, you have came to the right place to learn. This guide is designed to help you learn the very basics of League of Legends, so maybe, in a couple years time, you can contribute to the builds found on our home page, but for the time being id recommened following all the steps you will find here. If you do want to make the game more interesting, follow some of the builds that our community has created, i will advise however, some builds are not guaranteed wins and could upset a couple teammates.


To begin you will need to understand the lanes and roles within the game, we will go into more detail for each role however, this is a quick overview. Firstly, we have the top lane. Often regarded as the most boring lane as the least amount of action is likely to take place. This is a very beginner friendly lane, you will have little responsibilities so you can practice alot of the basics such as farming. Next we have the jungle, my personal favourite, the jungle is a very hard role, you are expected to be everywhere on the map at the same time, ganking, which is when you attack a lane with your teammates to try to secure a kill, as well as taking all objectives, all whilst keeping up with your own farming. This is probably not the best beginner role for an enjoyable experience however, how are you going to get good at a role if you never play it? The mid lane, everyone wants to be a mid laner, it can be the highest reward lane if played correctly, you have a lot of responsibilities as a mid laner and are the most vulnerable during the game. The bottom lane consits of both the ADC and support. You will most likely find that both the teammates want to blame each other and never take responsibility. The adc is the damage on the lane, they will farm and the supports job is to protect them the best they can. Some supports will also look for opportunites to make a play on the lane and try to get a kill for the adc.


Now we have covered a little about each lane/role, we will cover farming in the game, commonly known as 'csing'. As a laner, you will spend the majority of your time farming the minions that spawn and arrive on your lane. These minions will be your biggest source of exp and gold for the majority of the game so it is important you understand how to farm them correctly. As a new player, you will want to last hit the minions as much as possible. Last hitting is where you only ever attack the minions to make sure they die, you are not constantly auto attacking the minions. Constantly auto attacking the minions will lead to you pushing the lane into the enemy tower, where you are more vulnerable to ganks. If you are playing a melee into melee match-up, you will also be thinking about trading with the enemy when they are wanting to last hit a minion, this is the best time to try and auto attack them a couple of times as they cannot attack back until they have killed the minion. This is a rather difficult skill however it will come with time as you begin to recognise the enemys auto attack animation. If you are playing a ranged into melee matchup where you are the ranged, you will be wanting to pressure the enemy laner as much as possible, auto attacking them many times when they want to farm, just rememeber that you also should be farming the minions. You should also want to keep your distance from them, if you notice them walking towards you, it would be best to run away. If you are the melee into a ranged vs melee matchup, you will want to just grab as much minion gold as possible and wait for help from your teammates, it will be a hard matchup but you wont lose so long as you stay in exp range. If you are in a ranged vs ranged matchup, focus all your attention on the farm, chances are you wont be fighting much so you need to out farm your opponent to win the lane. We will cover farming as a jungler in our jungle section.


Please read through all sections we will post here, it is very important that you understand the role you will be playing, but also understand the roles your teammates will be playing. You will better undertsand the game this way and hopefully win more games.

Top Lane


The top lane, we described this lane as being the most boring lane, this is only because it should see the least amount of action within the first 15-20 minutes of the game. However, with some of the strongest champions within the game, you can have lots of fun rolling over the enemy if you have a good laning phase.

Firstly we will go over the responsibilities of the top laner. During the laning phase, as a top laner, you will need to be securing vision within the river and close to rift herald. This will protect you from ganks, as well as give valuable information to the team on the location of the enemy jungler. Also as a top laner you will be taking teleport most of the time, you can use this teleport to gank the bottom lane if the situation is there, for example if your bot lane has placed a ward deep into their lane and they are being pushed into tower, you might find the opportunity to teleport behind the enemy bot lane and assist your bot lane in killing the enemy team. This is a great use of teleport as it get multiple lanes ahead, just make sure you hurry back to your own lane to avoid losing too much exp or gold from minions. These are the responsibilities of a top laner during the laning phase of the game, beyond the laning phase, you will be playing as a team so all lanes and roles will be fighting together to take objectives as a team.

Secondly, we will cover the general game play. You should always be actively searching to fulfill the responsibilities of a top laner, however in the time where nothing can be achieved you will be wanting to farm as many of the minions as possible, whilst taking good trades with your opponenet. The better you farm than the enemy top laner, the quicker you will reach level six, which is a massive power spike for most champions, as well you will be able to buy more items earlier to give yourself power over your opponenet. If your enemy laner has left to back or assist another lane, be sure to quickly push your lane into the enemy tower, your enemy cannot gain any exp or gold from minions that die to the turret. For a portion of the game, the towers will have plating that can give you some gold, these can be great to get so long as you dont greed for them and die for it to either a gank or the enemy laner.

For the top lane, we recommend you play: Garen, Darius, Tahm Kench or Sett.
